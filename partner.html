Frequently Asked Questions

How is commission calculated?
Commission is calculated based on the actual paid amount generated by users through your promotion link, with a tax rate of 30%. For example, if a user purchases a $100 package, you will receive a $30 commission.
When can I withdraw?
You can apply for withdrawal when your account balance reaches $50. Withdrawals are typically processed within 3-5 business days, with support for PayPal, bank transfers, and other methods.
How long is the promotion link valid?
Your promotion link is permanently valid and will not expire. After a user registers through your link, all subsequent paid actions will generate commissions for you.
Can I promote to any region?
We support global promotion with no geographical restrictions. You can participate in our partner program and earn revenue regardless of your location.
